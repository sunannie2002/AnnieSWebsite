Why I admire Michelle Kwan

I admire Michelle Kwan because she is an inspiration. She has persevered through many challenges in her life to become one of the most decorated figure skaters. Growing up, her family did not have a lot of money and immigrated from Hong Kong, so her parents worked multiple jobs to afford her lessons and gear. Seeing how hardworking her parents are, Michelle Kwan was determined to persevere and become a champion.

Facts about Michelle Kwan

  • Michelle Kwan has won five world figure skating championships.
  • Michelle Kwan has competed in the Olympics two times.
  • Michelle Kwan has won two Olympic medals.
